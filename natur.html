Natur und Metamorphose

Location: Dieburg, online
Role: Web implementation with WP

The exhibition Nature and Metamorphosis - Digital Paths to an Ecology, showcased both at Schloss Fechenbach in Dieburg and online, we delve into the intricate convergence of humanity, technology, and nature. As part of the core team in the Master's program, my contribution to this collaborative project involved implementing the web presence.

The online exhibition showcased in a variety of artworks, such as installations, sound compositions, and 3D audio.
