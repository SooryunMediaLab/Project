StadtWerkStatt Medien: Getrennt Verbunden

Role and contributions:
Public Relations, Workshop design, Workshop operation

StadtWerkStatt Medien is an annual media workshop central to my Master's program. As part of the public relations team, I managed communications with partner institutions, handled invitations, and maintained the list of artists and institutions.


Event Details:
"Exploring the Dynamics of Being Apart Yet Connected Through Digital Media"

The event is organized by students from the Master’s program, aims to broaden the understanding of media aesthetics among both local and international residents. After the workshops, the creative works produced by participants are exhibited at the Schloss Fechenbach museum in an exhibition named “Apart Connected.” The digital social sculpture is showcased in an online exhibition addressing themes of isolation, connectivity, and the transformation of cultural and artistic practices following the pandemic.


Workshop: Compose Dieburg

About Workshop:
Stadtwerkstatt Medien is consists of several workshop sessions, where we gathered the outcomes to create a single art piece. The aim is to engage with local communities and make digital art more accessible to everyone. In the workshop Compose Dieburg, we focused on exploring the sonic environment and allowed participants to contribute to the art of sound composition.

Traditionally held at the Dieburg marketplace, the event moved to a virtual marketplace in 2021 due to the Corona Pandemic.
