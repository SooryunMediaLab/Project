Begegnungen in Dieburg

Location: Online, Dieburg
Role: Public Relations

"Exploring the Dynamics of Being Apart Yet Connected Through Digital Media"

StadtWerkStatt Medien is an annual media workshop central to the Master’s program in International Media Cultural Work. Traditionally held at the Dieburg marketplace, the event moved to a virtual marketplace in 2021 due to the Corona Pandemic.

The event is organized by students from the Master’s program, aims to broaden the understanding of media aesthetics among both local and international residents. After the workshops, the creative works produced by participants are exhibited at the Schloss Fechenbach museum in an exhibition named “Apart Connected.” The digital social sculpture is showcased in an online exhibition addressing themes of isolation, connectivity, and the transformation of cultural and artistic practices following the pandemic.


Workshop

Workshop: Compose Dieburg
Role: Workshop Planning

The online-workshop "Compose Dieburg" invites all visitors for one day to become acoustic designers of Dieburg. All participants will be able to listen to the authentical sounds of local places and rely on their individual preferences to rearrange them on the city map.

Are some places missing in the city?
Would it be nice to have a new Art gallery or Theater?
Everyone is free to experiment and create, not just a new look of the Dieburg map, but furthermore its overall sound composition.
