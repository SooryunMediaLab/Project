The City Story


Location: Seoul, Korea
Role: Event Management, Curation & Operation

The exhibition "Live and Leave" explored into the narrative of urban traces within contemporary society, exploring how cities, under the immense influence of gentrification, are constantly dismantled and reassembled.

It is aimed to take a look at the spaces and lives that have been marginalized or forgotten due to these changes. Challenging the reliance on digital mapping, it questions whether unrecognized spaces can truly remain hidden in the digital age. Through the revival of placemaking principles, 'Live, Leave' tried to reawaken the sensory experiences of urban inhabitants, highlighting the forgotten memories, the aesthetic and structural nuances of cityscapes, and the personal narratives that weave through the urban fabric.
