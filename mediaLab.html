The Only Way Out


Role and contributions:
Field recording, Audio technical assistant
Participated in field recording, stereo sound editing, and editing for 3D audio.
2nd Nachwuchspreis Medienkultur der Stadt Dieburg 2021


Project Details: The project is part of the "Fulldome Films in 3D Audio" seminar within the master's program.
Fulldome is a live-action film that explores visual, dramatic, and actor-specific processing in a 360° environment visually and acoustically.

The theme "Migration, Courage, Liberation" and the fairy tale "Hansel and Gretel" served as motifs for creating a multilog that includes scenic dialogues and environmental sounds in a 3D audio soundtrack.
