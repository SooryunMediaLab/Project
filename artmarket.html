Art Meets Commercial

Location: Seoul, Korea
Role: Art Product design

The project "Selling Sentiment"(Original: 감성팔이) was an art market project I participated in as part of a seminar assignment.
It was initially conceived as a feature for a magazine. In this project, I created art-commercial products by collecting anonymous participants' stories on online.

Each selected piece was given an emotionally resonant and exaggerated title, which I then inscribed on the envelope of a sealed letter to be sold. The objective was to captivate and entertain by assigning compelling and emotionally charged titles to the diverse range of submissions, effectively merchandising sentiment.
